Timeline of David Fong's Life:
  • David Fong was the son of Thomas Fong and May Yuk Yee
  • David grew up in Taishan, China
  • Born in 1932, the 3rd of 2 brothers and 2 sisters.
  • In 1952 David moved to San Francisco and attended SF City College where he majored in engineering.
  • After graduating David was hired by PG&E. He loved his job as he would often talk about the friends and wonderful times he had there. He proudly displayed the gold watch given to him for his many years of service at PG&E.
  • In 1967 David married Pauline Yee.
  • Pauline and David both worked at PG&E for the next 33 years until they both retired in 1994.
  • Sadly, in 2002 Pauline past away. This was a very sad period in his life
  • This lasted til 2006, where David married Wendy Lei, providing the love and happiness that was missing in his life.
  • Unfortunately, the last several years, David battled diabetes and even cancer, but thanks for the care and love from Wendy and friends and family, he was able to live a very happy life to the end.

Rest in peace
